The Experience

Hair that speaks

At our salon, we work with every hair type and texture. Natural, relaxed, loc'd, braided — we understand that your hair is deeply personal, and we approach every appointment with that in mind.

"Great hair doesn't happen by chance. It happens at Olakira."

Our stylists are skilled in protective styles, braiding techniques, colour services, and wash-and-go treatments. We take time to understand your vision before we begin — because the result should feel like you.

Our Salon Services

01
Wash & Style
A thorough cleanse, deep conditioning, and professional blowout or set — leaving your hair healthy, shiny, and styled to perfection.
02
Braiding & Protective Styles
From classic box braids to intricate feed-ins — our braiders work with precision and speed using two stylists where needed.

Natural Hair Care
Tailored treatments for coils, kinks, and curls — twist-outs, wash-and-go, banding, and more to celebrate your natural texture.

From bold single-process colour to subtle balayage and highlights — applied with care to protect your hair's integrity.

Relaxer Treatments
